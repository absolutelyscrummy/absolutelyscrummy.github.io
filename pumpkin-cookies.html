Ingredients 1 1/2 cup gluten free flour 1/4 tsp baking soda 1/4 tsp baking soda 2 tsp salt 1 tsp fine ground black pepper 1 tsp nutmeg 1 tsp cinnamon 1/2 cup olive oil 1/4 cup sugar 1/2 cup brown sugar 6 tbsp blotted pumpkin 1 tsp vanilla Oven: 350 - convection Combine all ingredients. Bake at 350 for 8 minutes, or until edges start to brown.
